Hephaestus

A rare-earth element index family
QueensGate Research · data as of —

Rare earths have no single price. The seventeen lanthanides-plus trade in opaque, desk-quoted oxide markets where a kilogram of lanthanum costs less than a coffee and a kilogram of terbium costs more than an ounce of gold. Hephaestus reduces that to three indices, each a different way of asking the same question: how exposed is the modern economy to this supply chain, and what is that exposure worth?

Every index below is a fixed-basket (Laspeyres) price index built from the same estimator. They differ only in the basket — the quantity vector that encodes the weighting. The three current levels are below; the figure and method follow.

1. Method

Each index is a fixed-quantity (Laspeyres) price index — the same construction as the S&P GSCI or a consumer price index. A representative basket of physical quantities is held constant and we track the changing cost of buying it, so a move in the index is pure price, not a rebalancing artefact:

$$ I_b(t) \;=\; 100 \times \frac{\sum_i q_i^{(b)}\, p_i(t)}{\sum_i q_i^{(b)}\, p_i(t_0)} $$

where \(p_i(t)\) is the oxide price of element \(i\) in month \(t\) (\$/kg REO, FOB China), \(q_i^{(b)}\) is the fixed basket quantity for index \(b\), and \(t_0\) is January 2010 (rebased to 100). The three indices share this equation and differ only in the basket \(q^{(b)}\):

  • QG-SCX (Supply-Chain Criticality, the flagship): \(q_i = C_i\, S_i\, E_i\), where \(C_i\) is global annual consumption, \(S_i\) is supply concentration (share of refined output from the single dominant jurisdiction, an HHI proxy), and \(E_i\) is economic criticality (end-use value, substitutability, defence relevance). Over-weights the fragile, hard-to-replace links.
  • QG-MAG (Permanent-Magnet basket): \(q_i = M_i\) for Nd, Pr, Dy, Tb and \(0\) otherwise, with \(M_i\) the magnet-sector consumption. Just the NdFeB metals that drive EV motors, wind turbines and precision-guided munitions.
  • QG-VAL (Market value / demand): \(q_i = C_i\), raw consumption in tonnes. The neutral cap-weight analogue — what the market physically buys.

Why a fixed basket

Value-weighting with live prices lets a spiking element inflate its own weight and double-count the move (a Paasche bias). Fixing quantities at their structural, consumption-based levels keeps each index an honest price signal. The trade-off — weights drift from current market value as prices move — is the right one for a research benchmark, and is stated rather than hidden.

2. Data

There is no live tape for rare-earth oxides; prices are published periodically by specialist desks behind paywalls. Hephaestus is built from dated public anchor prints at the finest resolution each market honestly supports — quarterly-to-monthly for the liquid magnet oxides (Nd, Pr, Dy, Tb) that dominate every index, denser through the 2010–11 spike and the 2021–22 rally, and sparser for thin markets that do not trade every month. The monthly grid between anchors is filled by log-linear interpolation. Intra-anchor volatility is deliberately not fabricated: a straight segment on a log axis means "not observed this month." The chart's texture is real structure — different elements turning in different months — not invented noise. Resolution therefore varies by liquidity, shown by the flag in Table 1:

  • reported — liquid, regularly-quoted oxide market; quarterly-to-monthly anchors that track published prints closely.
  • thin — genuine but illiquid and sparsely quoted; coarser anchors, wider uncertainty.
  • modeled — negligible traded volume (Sc, Tm); a documented estimate at coarse resolution.
  • promethium (Pm) has no commercial market — radioactive, half-life in years — and is excluded from every index.

Disclaimer. Hephaestus is a research and portfolio exercise, not investment advice, a tradable product, or a live data feed. Oxide prices are periodic public anchors with monthly interpolation, not tick data; absolute levels carry real uncertainty and thin/modeled series more so. The value is in the construction and the relative dynamics, which are reproducible from the method above.
